UNITING BLACK MINDS TO SEE BEYOND.

Our mission is to diversify perceptions of physics by inspiring, connecting and representing.

A mission that drives our fundamental role at this moment: to be an enlightening force that's changing the face of physics.

ABOUT US

We are a social enterprise made up of people from a variety of professions and specialisms all over the country who share a passion for physics and positive representations of the Black community.
