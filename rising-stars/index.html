Rising Stars

The Rising Stars Research School is a fully-funded three day career development workshop for UK-based physicists and engineers of Black heritage that looks to train the next-generation of research leaders.

The workshop will be modelled on the highly successful Rising Stars programme offered by MIT and will bring together 30 outstanding final year PhD students and postdocs from the UK in the fields of physics and engineering for an intensive three-day workshop dedicated to career development, improving access and participation in research. There will be activities directed both to those looking to start and pursue careers in research, as well as activities for those looking to further their careers beyond academia. The funded opportunity will take place at Imperial College London (South Kensington) on Wednesday 29th June - Friday 1st July and is for physicists and engineers of Black heritage.

Activities will include:

  • Panel Discussions
  • Writing Courses
  • Science Communication Sessions
  • Social Media Masterclass
  • CV Clinic
  • A Visit to the National Physics Laboratory
  • Networking Events
