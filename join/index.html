Join Us!

The Blackett Lab Family provides a space for UK based Black physicists to connect with each other. We act as an online community of support, interaction, and fun, and encourage members to get involved with lots of social, educational, and professional opportunities. Our primary platform to connect with each other is Facebook, though you can engage with us on Instagram and Twitter too.

The purpose of this short questionnaire is for you to introduce yourself to us, and for us to get to know you a bit more. If you have any questions about joining, or you’re using a browser that doesn’t support this form, feel free to contact us directly at community@theblackettlabfamily.com.
